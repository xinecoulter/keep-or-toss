Keep Or Toss?

  • Have I used this within the last two years?

  • Is this item enhancing my life?

  • Is this item good to have in an emergency situation?

  • Is this something I'll want my children to see one day?

  • Would it be too expensive to replace?

  • Does keeping this item make more work for me?

  • Would I move this to a new home?

  • Do I know someone who would appreciate this more than I do?
